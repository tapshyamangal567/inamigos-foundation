ABOUT INAMIGOS FOUNDATION

About Us

Founded in 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO committed to social impact and sustainable community development across India.

We work towards creating meaningful change through education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, youth development, and community support initiatives. Our programs are designed to empower underserved communities and create long-term impact through collaboration, compassion, and innovation.

Mission

To empower communities through education, sustainability, skill development, and social welfare initiatives that improve quality of life.

Vision

To build inclusive, self-reliant, and compassionate communities where people and the environment grow together.

Values

Compassion, integrity, collaboration, sustainability, inclusiveness, and commitment to social impact.
